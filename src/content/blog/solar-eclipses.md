I also recently built a frontend app for visualizing solar eclipses.

The app is available here:

[Open the solar eclipses app](/apps/solar-eclipses)

Solar eclipses are an especially nice subject for visualization because they combine geometry, orbital motion, scale, and timing in a way that is both visually appealing and scientifically interesting.

This app is intended to make it easier to explore:

- how the Sun, Moon, and Earth line up
- how eclipse paths and visibility depend on geometry
- how a visual model can make the underlying physics more intuitive

If the topic interests you, the best place to start is the app itself.
